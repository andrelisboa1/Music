Biography

André Lisboa (b. 1998)

Earlier Years

André was born on the 29th of January, 1998, on the city of Setúbal, Portugal.
When he was 9 years old, he joined Academia de Música e Belas Artes Luísa Todi (translated: Luísa Todi Academy of Music and Fine Arts).

Higher Education

André started his college education in Composition in 2016, at Lisbon School of Music.
During his bachelor's, he studied under composers and educators such as Sérgio Azevedo, Luís Tinoco, João Madureira, Carlos Marecos, Carlos Caires, José Luís Ferreira† and Jaime Reis.
